The year 2021-22 proved to be an eventful and challenging year for the Akhil Bhartiya Antyodaya Sansthan. Major focus has been on the increasing empower of education,Self Help Group, Health and meditation of accredited organisations. The organization has also entered into a partnership with India for its partner's accreditation under ABAS Norms. Akhil Bhartiya Antyodaya Santhan participated in various national consultation and orientation workshop for its members and NGOs.
